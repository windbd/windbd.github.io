About me [Short bio]

I am currently a tenure-track SUTD Assistant Professor at the Information Systems Technology and Design (ISTD) pillar, Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD). Before this, I was a research scientist at Alibaba Group Singapore. I received my Ph.D. from The Chinese University of Hong Kong.

My research interests lie broadly in natural language processing and machine learning, though most of my research are centered around large language models these days. I am passinate to advance NLP models that are inclusive, supporting diverse languages and cultures (e.g., multilingual large language models), while also trustworthy through techniques that improve understanding, safety and robustness of language models.

Research projects I'm working on / plan to work on:
  • [Multilingual] Understanding and enhancing multilingual capabilities of LLMs (SeaLLMs Project)
  • [Safety] Ensuring safety of LLMs from development to deployment (MultiJail)
  • [Evaluation] Comprehensive and fair evaluation of foundation models (M3Exam, Auto-Arena)
  • [Accessiblity] Making LLMs more accessible to general users, through model compression or small-large model collaboration
